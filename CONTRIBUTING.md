## Permitted Contributions
# Contributing to Somatic Field Systems

This repository is **not open-contribution by default**.

---

## Who May Contribute

• Hollow House Institute maintainers  
• Approved researchers  
• Licensed collaborators  

Unapproved pull requests may be closed without review.

---

## Contribution Rules

All contributions must:

• Be non-extractive  
• Avoid pathologizing language  
• Respect nervous-system safety  
• Include clear intent and scope  
• Pass governance review  

---

## Prohibited Contributions

Do not submit:

• Commercial optimization logic  
• Behavioral prediction models  
• Scoring, ranking, or profiling systems  
• Medical or diagnostic frameworks  

---

## Process

1. Open an Issue describing intent  
2. Await governance approval  
3. Submit a focused PR  
4. Pass ethics & scope review  

Violations result in permanent exclusion.
